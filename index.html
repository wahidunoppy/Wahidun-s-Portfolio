About

Quality isn't a phase, it's a mindset.

I'm a Software QA Engineer based in Dhaka, working with teams across product, design, and engineering to ship software that actually holds up in production. My background spans manual exploratory testing, automated regression suites, and API/contract testing — I move between all three depending on what a release actually needs, not what's trendy.

I started in QA after watching one too many "quick fixes" cause cascading production incidents. Since then I've built test strategies for fintech, e-commerce, and SaaS products, and I still get a small thrill every time a regression test catches something before a user does.

🎯 Risk-based test planning that prioritizes what actually breaks
